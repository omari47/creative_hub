About This Workshop

This comprehensive photography masterclass will elevate your understanding of light and composition to create stunning images in any setting. Learn the technical and artistic principles that professional photographers use to capture compelling visual stories.

Through practical demonstrations and hands-on exercises, you'll develop the skills to control light, frame your subjects effectively, and create images with strong visual impact.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding different light sources and how they affect your images
  • Mastering the exposure triangle for perfect lighting in any situation
  • Creating dramatic lighting effects using simple equipment
  • Applying the rule of thirds, golden ratio, and other compositional guidelines
  • Using lines, shapes, and patterns to create visual interest
  • Developing your personal style through effective composition choices

Requirements

  • Any camera (DSLR, mirrorless, or smartphone with manual controls recommended)
  • Basic understanding of camera operation (exposure, focus, etc.)
  • Computer with internet connection for the online session
  • Optional: tripod, external flash or continuous lighting if available
